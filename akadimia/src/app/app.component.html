What is Akadimía ?

Akadimia is a pool of highly talented instructors and tutors to meet the need of focussed private coaching. Whether summer camps, story-telling teachers, private tutors, coach or motivational speakers , Akadimia provides the end user with a platform to evaluate, choose and execute time bound targetted training session without worrying about the hassles of logistics.

Do you have any requirement for a specific training ?

Get in touch with us today.

We strive to find out the best in class teachers suited to your location, subject and time that is within your budget.

You can also opt to be a trainer yourself and showcase your talent.

How does it all work?

Register on our platform as a Corporate or Individual and search for trainings you are looking for along with your location and time slots

Our intelligent search engine returns the tutors based on their talent scores who are available to take up the assignment

You can then opt to have a physical or online demo session

Once you are comfortable to go ahead you complete the formalities and leave it to us to execute the rest of the stuff
